My Journey

I started my journey in the world of technology with a passion for coding and problem-solving. Over the years, I've honed my skills in various programming languages and frameworks, allowing me to build robust and scalable applications. Since the age of 10, I've been an avid computer user, learning how to mount ISOs, and participating in peer-to-peer networks. My first encounter with the art of computer programming was during a class where I would learn about Object-Oriented programming in C++. Since then, I've developed an avid interest in building software, with an interest in software engineering, databases, and information security.
